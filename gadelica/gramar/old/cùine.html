Cùine

Cùin a bha an cèilidh ann?
'When was the party?'

Cùin a bhrist thu an iuchair?
'When did you break the key?'

Cùin a thill thu dhachaigh?
'When did you return home?'

Cùin a chaidh iad a-mach?
'When did they go out?'

Cùin a ghabhas sinn ar dìnnear?
'When will we have our dinner?'

Cùin a ghabhas sibh an t-òran?
‘When will you sing the song?’

Cùin a gheibh iad am plèan?
'When will they get the plane?'

Cùin a thòisich sibh air sgrìobhadh?
'When did you begin writing?'

Cùine a dhùisgeadh tu anns a’ mhadainn?
‘When did you used to wake up in the morning?’

A bheil fios agaibh cùin a thàinig an trèan?
'Do you know when the train came?'
