Is (copula)

Subject is (emphatic) personal pronoun

  • Is mise Iain. ‘I am John.’
  • Is esan Seumas. ‘He is James.’
  • Is iadsan Màiri agus Calum. ‘They are Mary and Calum.’
  • Is ise an tidsear. ‘She is the teacher.’
  • Is esan mo bhràthair. ‘He is my brother.’

Subject is definite noun phrase

  • Is i/e Anna an tidsear. ‘Ann is the teacher.’
  • Is e mo athair am ministear. ‘My father is the minister.’
  • Is e an sagart do bhràthair. ‘The priest is your brother.’
  • Is e seo an tidsear. ‘This is the teacher.’
  • Is i/e siud Màiri. ‘That is Mary.’
