Ciamar

Ciamar a tha sibh?
'How are you?'

Ciamar a bha an cèilidh?
'How was the party?'

Ciamar a bhrist thu an iuchair?
'How did you break the key?'

Ciamar a dhùineas tu an dòras?
‘How will you close the door?’

Ciamar a chòrd an cèilidh riut?
'How did you enjow the party?'

Leigidh sinn fios thugad uaireigin air an ath-sheachdain ciamar a chaidh dhut anns an agallamh.
'We’ll let you know sometime next week how you fared in the interview.'
