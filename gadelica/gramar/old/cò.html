Cò

Cò thusa?
'Who are you?'

Cò iadsan?
'Who are they?'

Cò a bhris an uinneag?
'Who broke the window?'

Cò a dhùin an doras?
'Who closed the door?'

Cò a tha a’ gabhail an òrain?
‘Who is singing the song?’

Cò a chuireas a-mach an cat?
'Who put the cat out?'

Cò a chuidicheas mi?
‘Whom shall I help?’

Cò a bhios a’ seinn aig a’ chèilidh?
'Who will be singing at the party?'

Cò a rinn seo?
'Who did this?'

Cò a tha gar n-iarraidh?
‘Who wants us?’

Cò às a tha thu?
‘Where are you from?’

Cò leis a tha an leabhar?
'Whose is the book?'

Cò ris a bhruidhinn Iain?
'Who did John speak to?'

Cò dha a dh’innis Anna an naidheachd?
'Who did Ann tell the news to?'

Cò aige a tha an iuchair?
'Who has the key?'

Cò dheth/dha a dh’fhaighnich thu?
'Who did you ask?'

Cò dhiubh a b’ fheàrr leat tì no cofaidh?
'Which would you prefer, tea or coffee?'

Cò mheud ugh?
'How many eggs?'

Chan eil fios agam cò a chuir an litir.
'I don't know who sent the letter.'

Innis dhomh cò a bha ann.
'Tell me who was there.'
